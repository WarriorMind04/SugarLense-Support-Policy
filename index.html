Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data shared?

No. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

How do I delete my data?

Simply delete the app from your device. All stored data will be permanently removed.

Is Sugar Lens a medical device?

No. Sugar Lens is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical decisions.
