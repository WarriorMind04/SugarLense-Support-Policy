Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data shared?

No. All personal and health data is stored locally on your device. Sugar Lens does not transmit personal health data to external servers.

Does the app use AI services?

Food recognition is performed locally using Apple CoreML.

The app retrieves nutritional information from the USDA FoodData Central database. No personal or health data is transmitted.

How do I delete my data?

Simply delete the app from your device. All stored data will be permanently removed.

Is Sugar Lens a medical device?

No. Sugar Lens is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional.
